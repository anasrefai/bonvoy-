Our Story

BonVoy was born in Amman with one simple belief: every celebration deserves something extraordinary. We bake fresh cakes, rich brownies, and indulgent cookies — crafted from premium ingredients, delivered to your door.

As a sister brand of @cakeh.jo, we bring 132K followers' worth of trust and passion into every order. Whether it's a birthday, a Tuesday, or just because — BonVoy delivers joy.

We currently serve Amman and ship across Jordan. Every order is handled with care from our kitchen in Alrabieh.
